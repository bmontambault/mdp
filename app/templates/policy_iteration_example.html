In policy iteration we alternate between evaluating the value function given a fixed policy and updating the policy given the value function.

As an example we look at an environment where we recieve a reward of 1 in the states (0,0) and (3,3), and no reward in any other states. The states (2,2), (2,3), and (2,4) are blocked off and cannot be traversed. We initialize the value function to be 0 at each state (right), and initialize the policy to be random (left).
